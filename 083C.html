MDL Fan 5 PCB stuffola¶

This note was written up 10 Nov 2021 15:55 E for Dan M.

Just to make the revisions of the datasheet-like document and picture of PCBs wrapped for air isolation easy to manage.

  • Dan Kolis

Five PCB SN 53-2004¶

Five of these are sealed up ready to be made.

It's been some odd months.

Fig. 1 Five of these in a sealed bag¶

These are Through hole Double sided PCBs.

As far as I can tell, they came out great.

I have no parts for them. The little bits of unknown circuitry have been tested on a breadboard.

The nomenclature on the ART says ``MDL Solutions'', on each of them.

Computer Simulation of the PCB¶

The art shows the parts placement. There is provision for either tabs or poke in connectors, or even both. That's why the PCB is larger. But of course its also larger as it does 12 set points.

Fig. 2 Computer simulation of the PCB¶

It has a voltage regulator so wiring drop from 24 VDC has no effect on accuracy of output.

And a control point, could be for a flow switch.
